I have recently gotten into paddle boarding which has been awesome since I have lakes close to me and if I wanted to go on a little weekend adventure to paddle board, the beach is just a 3 hour drive. Besides paddle boarding, I enjoy watching movies over reading a book. When there is a movie I want to watch that is based off a book, I won't read the book since I know it will ruin the movie for me. If you have ever read 'Twilight' and then watched the movies, you know what I mean.

I love travelling! I want to show my daughter the world so every year for her birthday, rather than doing a party with her friends, I plan a trip to a new place. Most of these trips have in the US since she's still young and I don't think she will do well on a long flight right now with the energy she has.
